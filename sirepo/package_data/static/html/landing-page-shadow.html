Shadow3

Shadow3 simulates all types of charged particle accelerators with a wide range of features, including support for nonlinear optimization and design. elegant is freely available. A user manual is provided, and there is also an active online user forum. The code is primarily supported by Michael Borland and colleagues at the Argonne National Laboratory. Contributors include M. Borland, M. Carla, N Carmignani
Sirepo development has been supported by the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science, under multiple awards. The Shadow3 interface is supported by the Office of Basic Energy Sciences under Award No. DE-SC0011237.

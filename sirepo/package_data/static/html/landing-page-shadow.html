Shadow3 is a ray optics code with many sophisticated features for X-ray beamlines.

The Shadow3 source code is available on GitHub.

Shadow3 is primarily supported by Manuel Sanchez del Rio and colleagues at ESRF.

The Sirepo GUI is primarily supported by the development team at RadiaSoft LLC.
Publications
  • C. Welnak, G.J. Chen and F. Cerrina, "SHADOW: A synchrotron radiation and X-ray optics simulation tool," Nuclear Instruments and Methods in Physics Research Volume A347, p. 344 (1994).
  • M. Sanchez del Rio, N. Canestrari, F. Jiang and F. Cerrina, "SHADOW3: a new version of the synchrotron X-ray optics modelling package," J. Synchrotron Radiat., p. 708 (2011).
Sirepo development has been supported by the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science, under multiple awards. The Shadow3 interface is supported by the Office of Basic Energy Sciences under Award No. DE-SC0011237.
